Pizza

This is a recipe for homemade Pizza Dough, easy and delicious, in less than 1 hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Warm Water (around 110°F or 45°C)
  • 1 package of Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of White Sugar
  • 2 and a half cups of Bread Flour
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
